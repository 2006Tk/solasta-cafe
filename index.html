The Sanctuary

An Earthy, Fairy-Lit Hideaway Far From Traffic Noise

Tucked away on the Faridabad-Gurgaon Road in Gwal Pahari, **Solasta Cafe** is an outdoor oasis designed to help you reconnect with nature. Our multi-level wooden decks are surrounded by native trees, flower bushes, and glowing warm string lights.

We specialize in rustic comfort dining. Our kitchen features a massive outdoor brick oven where we bake thin-crust wood-fired pizzas, smoky roasted chicken skewers, and sweet warm skillet cookies.
